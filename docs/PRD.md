# Vision

ShipIntel is a logistics intelligence platform designed to help businesses, e-commerce sellers, and individuals make smarter shipping decisions.

Instead of being another logistics blog, ShipIntel provides practical tools such as calculators, courier comparisons, delivery estimators, logistics guides, and industry resources in a single platform.

Our long-term vision is to become the most trusted logistics information platform in India.

---

# Mission

To simplify logistics through accurate information, interactive tools, and data-driven insights.

---

# Problem Statement

Finding reliable logistics information is difficult.

Users often have to search multiple websites to find:

* Shipping charges
* Courier comparisons
* Delivery estimates
* Volumetric weight calculations
* Packaging recommendations
* Courier coverage
* Logistics terminology

Existing websites either provide outdated information or focus only on one courier company.

ShipIntel solves this by bringing everything together.
